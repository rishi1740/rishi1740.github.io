Databases    >    

DBMS this the easiest part in Gate exam cse .

When you are reading database for first time give sufficient time for understanding all the concepts in

databases because these are easy to understand.

Database questions can be answered quickly compared to other subject questions. Preparing this subject

can save the time in the exam and also easy to score good marks in an average.

Lecture videos of IITM might help for D.B.M.S

Book by Raghuramkrishnan is good for physical database organisation RA, TRC , SQL. Use solution manual

if required.

Normalization is an important part and very easy once understood and it could be solved relatively

quick. More chances of Finding normal form , finding candidate keys, decomposition of relation can be

asked in gate exam.

Prepare SQL Queries  with select clause with its properties well
 

Practice joins ,Finding View and Conflict serializability lock based, Two phase, time stamp and graph based

protocal i.e. deadlock and starvation freedom.

Navathe book is good for normalization part, ER diagrams. Navathe book is also good for physical

database organization, transaction management and Normalization.

After reading topics from korth book you can stick to navathe because it has lot of techniques that are not

anywhere else

.

Revise different concepts of databases and practice questions to get conceptual clarity.Database is a

relatively easier subject and questions from this subject should not take much time to answer.

Databases: Database System Concepts by Korth, 6th edition . This book contains details and as well as

lucid also well structured and explains the concept clearly.It doesnt have much on MySQL or Oracle but

covers a large set of theory and implementation aspects of databases.Use solution manual if required.

Korth book has all points required for GATE preparation good set of examples to understand database

concepts like normalization, transactions, and SQL.

It also includes real world illustrations about how a concept is being used and implemented. Everywhere

diagrams are given for easier understanding of how databases work.

The database design part in korth is difficult so you should read it several times in order to understand

things clearly.

Relational, tuple, SQL queries covers maximum weightage.
 

Normalization, Transactions, Relational algebra, SQL, ER-diagrams are relatively easy.
