Theory of Computation Gate CSE

TOC consist of 8-10 % weightage in gate cse.

It is one of the easiest subject to score if you have fully understood the concepts.

50 percent weightage is from finite automata from TOC so study it in detail so solve questions of finding

the no. of states ,NFA, DFA, epsilon conversions , closure properties ,special focus on finding regular

expressions and mealy , moorey machines and their conversions.

Practice more on simplification of context free grammar ,pushdown automata ,and find category of

language of grammar ,expressive power of different languages NP-Completeness,Properties of Recursive

and Recursive Enumerable Languages and Turing machines, Undecidability.
 

Turing Machine is also asked in interviews and its expressive power .

Questions on pumping lemma and its length .

Michel Sipser book contains advanced concepts read it at last good for Un-decidability, Recursive and

Recursively Enumerable languages, NP-completeness.

Ullman book contains good concepts on homomorphism and languages and most recommended for gate

cse.

Peter Linz book has all the basics and introduction to the theories of formal language and automata and

using mathematical expression for better understanding of the students focusing only on the concepts.It is

easy to understand but turing machine is tricky.

Regular languages, Undecidability covers a lot of portion.

Regular expressions, closure properties is relatively easy.

Revise topics such as Finite Automata, Regular Expressions, Pumping lemma, Context-free languages and

grammars.

Theory of Computation by Ullman is good for beginner you should concentrate well as it has some good points.

Free video lectures on youtube by Ravindrababu is awesome on this topic.


 

Also lecture videos of Prof. Kamala Krithivasan in NPTEL.


 

and Shai Simson at youtube is good too for reference.

You should try book by cohen and martin too.
