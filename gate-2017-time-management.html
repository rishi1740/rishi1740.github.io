    Gate 2017 Time Management

After starting of 5th semester of your graduation course it’s the best time to start

preparation of your GATE examination.

This is the perfect timing for you to start your preparation by shrinking your daily schedule

to give time for preparation for the said exam.

Preparing Engineering Mathematics and General Aptitude always easy to score in GATE

Exam compared to technical subjects.

Programming & Data Structure, Computer Networks, Theory of computation, and Operating Systems carry more marks in GATE Exam. So give more priority for preparing high weightage subjects for GATE 2017 Exam.

Reading toppers blogs, plan own strategy,deciding books for a couple of days aspirants

should read all old toppers blog / experiences regarding GATE preparation ther exist a

repetitive pattern along their preparation .

You should prepare the whole syllabus and it such a way that with rigourous MONTHLY

GOALS it gets completed and time would be left for revision , mock test and solving

previous year question papers.

You can practice three full length and five topic-wise tests every week and recall all

concepts while taking those tests.

Gate exam is usually held in between 10 -15 February so finish the Gate syllabus in

september or october.
 

Finish syllabus thoroughly as soon as possible.

If you are strong in basics, no need to waste time in advanced topics, just adhere to the

syllabus (most important).
