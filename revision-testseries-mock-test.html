     Revision Test Series And Mock Test

Test Series:

A well structured and good quality Test Series is a must for continuously evaluating your

performance and learning new concepts.

When it comes to Full Length Mock exams , first of all you should have finished one

iteration of studying everything completely to start giving mock exams.

Start solving exercises at the end of chapter.
 

Watch video lectures for 1-2 hours, read for 2 hours and solve for 2-3 hours
 

Once you get the grasp solve previous year questions of that topics, topicwise not

subjectwise.
 

Give tests on that chapter on same day itself.
 

Study a particular topic and attempt the past 10 years question papers for that topic only.

Take a test everyday if possible or at least once in 2-3 days and prepare an exam tactic

based on your experience.

While solving problems, students must have a balance between speed & accuracy.

Allot only last 10 days to revise the syllabus and a practice paper daily along with revision.

The more you practice in the last period before exam, the more confident you will get and

more accurate with the timing during last 3 hours of examination.

Daily, take one online test [subject-wise] , followed by its analysis and then correct the mistakes.

Solve recent previous year GATE questions two to three times.
 

Practice previous year GATE questions topic-wise daily.
 

This period helps identify areas of improvement and identify skipped topics.

Revisions also included revisiting the mistakes again and again so that you don’t repeat

them in FINAL EXAM.

PLAN:
 

1st week : Revision + 1 Full test*
2nd week: 2 online subject tests* (daily) + 1 FULL test*
3rd & 4th weeks: Online tests* [subject-wise] + 2 FULL test* + Previous year GATE papers ( with corrections)
During this phase, improve your speed.Try to solve the ‘full syllabus’ test in 2 and a half hours so that in the remaining half an hour you can go through it again.

PLAN:
 

1st week – Revision + solve GATE Previous year papers again and do written corrections for mistakes + 1 MOCK test*.
2nd week – 3 online subject tests daily with corrections + 1 MOCK TEST*
3rd week – Revision + 2 MOCK TEST*
4th week – Previous year GATE papers once again (with corrections): 3 papers daily + 2 mock tests.

Solve General Aptitude five questions daily.

It doesn’t mean simply taking the test. Instead, take the test – analyze it – identify your

weaknesses and work on them.

MOST IMPORTANT – Revisions. Keep revising periodically whatever you have learned. E.g. if  you learn something today, revisit it after a week, then after 15 days and then once in a 

month.
